globoesporte.com

Since 2011, I have been working as software developer in globoesporte.com and sportv.com, both written in Python/Django. The web sites are, respectively, the first and second top-rated sports portals in Brazil. In these projects, in addition to the Back End, I also have developed the Front End, consolidating the knowledge of jQuery and CSS and Semantic Web, used for relationship content.

UFC Combate

Besides all technologies used by globoesporte.com, we focused on technologies to improve page rank, such as using HTML5 and Schema.org tags.

Cartola FC

Cartola FC, a SporTV.com's fantasy game, works with data about users and leagues that have hundreds, or even thousands, of users. It also has statistics about each player of the Brazilian soccer league. In each round, there is a process we call "market update" in which the players' scores are calculated, the users' scores are calculated according to the team he put together and finally the leagues are ranked.
